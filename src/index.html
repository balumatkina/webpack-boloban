About
us

Opened on March 1985, the Unknown Aero Club has evolved and embodied the ideas of countless people who love to fly. The aviation organization has been repeatedly transformed by interests, changed its format and status, while not changing its key values: to create aviation and aviators, to develop aviation professions, to make the sky accessible for everyone.

Flights in the aero club are carried out in several directions, including training of civilian pilots, sports training, flights of private pilots for their own purposes, festivals and other events, flights for the ejection of parachutists.

The club trains athletes, instructors, and judges, organize and hold competitions, mass cultural and career orientation events. When the weather conditions are favorable, the flights at the aerodrome are holding the whole year. The active aviation season lasts from April till October.
